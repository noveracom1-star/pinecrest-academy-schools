Our Story

Founded in 1985, Pinecrest Academy began as a visionary project by a group of educators passionate about creating a learning environment that would nurture not just academic excellence, but also character and leadership.

What started as a small institution with just 50 students has now grown into a premier educational establishment serving over 1,200 students across multiple campuses. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation while staying true to our core values.

Throughout our history, we've remained committed to our founding principle: to provide an education that prepares students not just for exams, but for life. Our alumni network includes leaders in various fields across the globe, a testament to our enduring impact.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape everything we do at Pinecrest Academy

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible global citizens and lifelong learners. We are committed to academic excellence, character development, and the holistic growth of every student.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that sets the standard for academic excellence and character development. We aspire to create a community of learners who will become leaders and change-makers, making significant contributions to society.

Our Journey

Key milestones in Pinecrest Academy's history of educational excellence

Foundation Established

Pinecrest Academy was founded by Dr. Evelyn Thompson with a vision to create a holistic educational institution focusing on both academic excellence and character development.

1985

First Graduating Class

Our first cohort of students graduated with outstanding results, setting a precedent for academic excellence that would become our tradition.

1990

Campus Expansion

We expanded our campus facilities to include state-of-the-art science laboratories and a modern library to support our growing student population.

1998

STEM Program Launch

Introduced our specialized STEM program with advanced facilities, establishing Pinecrest as a leader in science and technology education.

2005

International Recognition

Received accreditation from international educational bodies, opening up global opportunities for our students and faculty.

2015

Digital Transformation
