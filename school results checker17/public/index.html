About Pinecrest Academy

Founded in 1985, Pinecrest Academy has been a beacon of educational excellence for over three decades. Our school was established with a vision to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Located in the heart of the city, our school spans over 25 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds, featuring state-of-the-art facilities designed to enhance the learning experience.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them to become responsible global citizens and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that sets the standard for academic excellence and character development.
