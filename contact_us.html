Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Pinecrest Academy

How do I apply for admission?

Our admission process begins with submitting an online application form. You can find the application portal on our Admissions page. Our admissions team will then contact you to guide you through the next steps.

What are the school fees?

School fees vary by grade level and program. For detailed information about tuition and other fees, please contact our admissions office at admissions@pinecrestacademy.edu or call +2349133719170.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional students. We also have financial aid programs for families who qualify. Contact our admissions office for more information about eligibility and application procedures.

What are your school hours?

Regular school hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Friday. We also offer after-school programs and extracurricular activities that extend until 5:00 PM.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we offer transportation services for students across Benin City and surrounding areas. Our fleet of school buses is equipped with safety features and supervised by trained staff.

How can I schedule a campus tour?

You can schedule a campus tour by contacting our admissions office via phone or email. We conduct tours on weekdays between 9:00 AM and 3:00 PM, and we also host open house events throughout the year.

Ready to Learn More?

Whether you're interested in admissions, have questions about our programs, or want to visit our campus, we're here to help you every step of the way.
