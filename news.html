Pinecrest Students Win National Science Fair with Innovative Water Purification System

A team of Pinecrest Academy students took home the top prize at the National Science Fair with their groundbreaking water purification system that uses solar energy and locally sourced materials. The project, developed by seniors Aisha Mohammed, Tunde Okafor, and Fatima Bello, impressed judges with its practical application and potential to impact communities with limited access to clean water.

Varsity Basketball Team Clinches Regional Championship in Thrilling Overtime Victory

In a nail-biting finish that had spectators on the edge of their seats, our Varsity Basketball Team secured the Regional Championship with a 78-75 overtime victory against our long-time rivals. Captain Emeka Okonkwo scored the game-winning basket with just 2.3 seconds remaining, capping off an incredible season that saw the team go undefeated in conference play.
